1983

    The journey of Napa started
  • Where did the name Napa come from?
  • Beximco Pharma came out of the traditional generic/indication based name and set a new trend.
  • NAPA comes from the chemical name of Paracetamol
  • N-Acetyl-P-Aminophenol
  • Uniqueness in naming gives Napa a distinguished identity from the competition.

1983-90

    Napa Holds only ~3% MS and was growing at a slow rate in MNC dominated Paracetamol market.

1990-92

But Beximco was always with the good practice and ensured good quality.

    The Paracetamol tragedy.
  • Diethylene glycol and propylene glycol are diluents. DEG is less expensive and severe hepatotoxic. Some small companies gave diethylene glycol instead of propylene glycol to make extra profit but this unethical practice brought serious hazards among children. Between 1990 and 1992, 429 children developed kidney failure, and most of them died after being given Paracetamol syrup contaminated with diethylene glycol. The outbreak forced the government to ban the sale of Paracetamol elixirs in December 1992.

1992

Such integrity of product and approach helped Beximco stand out the competition and brought huge success in terms of market share and brand image.

    Introduction of Paracetamol suspension.
  • After the Paracetamol tragedy, many of the big players stopped producing syrup and switched to suspension, except Beximco Pharma.
  • It was only Beximco Pharma to stand strong and stick with its syrup with confidence.

In course of time, this confidence was permanently embedded in doctors’ mind that took Napa a step ahead over the competition.

  • Reformulation of Napa tablet and the birth of an unprecedented, unbelievable formulation with 19 sec disintegration time.
  • Beximco was too confident with this phenomenal formulation to launch the first live demonstration campaign: Campaign 19 seconds.

Beximco gained the leadership in terms of selling for the 1st time during this period surpassing MNCs.

  • After the: Campaign 19 sec was a mega hit, Beximco got a big motivation and gained huge market share.

1997

This type of innovation in technology and formulation differentiated Napa from the competition

    Technology barrier: introduction of Napa Suppository.
  • The trendsetter Beximco brought in another innovation for the 1st time in Bangladesh. BPL launched Napa suppository 250 mg and 500 mg. At those days, it was a technological breakthrough that was difficult to imitate.

1995-98

    Napa became number #1 doctors’ choice in OTC analgesic in Bangladesh

2001-02

    Outbreak of Dengue in Bangladesh.

  • Dengue fever Outbreak in 2001 had a crucial role in the antipyretic-analgesic market dynamics. Dengue fever expedited the decline of Nimesulide market and gave Paracetamol market a new shape.

2002

The integrity in approach made BPL more credible and held Napa be the most reliable and most trusted Brand.

    The Nimesulide Fad.

  • Most of the leading companies launched Nimesulide.
  • Some prominent companies started to promote Nimesulide -“Simply better than Paracetamol”.

    • BPL was the only major company strongly stick to Paracetamol and promoted evidence based facts on Nimesulide to doctors.

    • In 2001-02, when Dengue out broke in Bangladesh, Nimesulide manufacturers fell in real danger as it was reported to cause hemorrhagic death to dengue patients. Paracetamol was the only choice in Dengue fever as per WHO guideline.

    • Drug Administration, after receiving complaints and study reports from physicians, banned Nimesulide in 2002.


2003

    Launching of Napa Drop.

  • Considering the dosage convenience Beximco Pharma launched Napa Pediatric drop for the 1st time in Bangladesh.
  • It opened up a new era for managing pain and fever in neonates and infants with easy dosing.

2005

Napa became more popular in general people and gained huge OTC advantage.

    Family pack concept.

  • In 2005, BPL launched a range of line extensions- a new pack of 100ml along with regular 60ml syrup, 60ml & 50ml Suspension and 500mg suppository.
  • Made a landmark Family pack concept (a wide Napa range for full family) and started OTC and DTC promotion.

    Brand extension: Introduction of Napa Extra.

  • Beximco Pharmaceuticals introduces launched a new Paracetamol combination product (Paracetamol+ Caffeine) which shows stronger action and very much effective in high fever and headache.
  • Napa Extra was a big hit in the subsequent years and currently it is the no.1 brand in paracetamol category.

2010

    Brand extension with new dual granulation technology.

  • Beximco Pharma introduces dual granulation technology in paracetamol formulation for the 1st time in Bangladesh. This dual granulation technology ensures both rapid onset of action and extended action up to 8 hour. Thus it has become a good choice in managing chronic mild to moderate pain.

2011

Napadol quickly made up its own market successfully and took the brand Napa to surgical section.

    Brand extension: innovation in pain management.

  • Paracetamol is indicated only in mild to moderate pain. Until 2011, there was no brand in Napa range to address acute pain states.
  • In 2011 BPL launched an innovative combination of Paracetamol and tramadol to cover up the acute pain section-

    The brand is NapaDol.

  • NapaDol was positioned in-

    Acute post-surgical pain and Osteoarthritis flare.

  • NapaDol was a mega hit because of its better pain managing capacity over Tramadol or Paracetamol and lesser records of vomiting tendency associated with Tramadol.

2012

Beximco came up with new combination of Paracetamol and methionine. But after the combination was banned internationally we stopped its manufacturing.

    Line extension: Exploring untapped areas

  • Until 2012, Napa had no room in purely hospital setting. Beximco Pharma launched Napa IV which is very much effective in pre-, during and post- surgery pain management. Napa IV is used as the adjunct to opioid or NSAIDs in post-operative pain management which ensures better outcome.

2018

We named our faster Napa as Napa Rapid.

    Beximco came up with new formulation technology: Actizorb technology

  • This technology ensures faster disintegration. Disintegraiton starts within just 2 seconds and complete disintegration occurs within two minutes.
  • Beximco also launched Napa One. It is 1g paracetamol formulation. Beximco pharma introduces it for the dosage convenience of patients when they need to take two 500mg tablet of paracetamol.
