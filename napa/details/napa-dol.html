Napadol

Synergy, for superior pain relief




Combination of Paracetamol and Tramadol exerts a synergistic effect and subsides mutual side effects.

Paracetamol

1000 mg

Non opioid analgesics

peripheral blockage of pain impulse generation


What does it contain?

  1. It contains Paracetamol 325md and tramadol 37.5mg.

What it is for?

  1. NapaDol® is indicated for the symptomatic treatment of moderate to severe pain.

It can be prescribed for

  1. Post-operative pain
  2. Painful arthritis flare
  3. Trauma
  4. Moderate to severe pain of any origin

What are its features?

  1. Added napa one and Napa Rapid.
  2. The use of 25% less tramadol in the combination product reduces the incidence of tramadol-related adverse events, while the addition of paracetamol reduces the onset time of analgesia and improves the degree of analgesia.
  4. Onset of action
    1. NapaDol 17 min Paracetamol 18 min Tramadol 51 min.
  5. Duration of action
    1. NapaDol 5 hour Paracetamol 3hour 5 min Tramadol 2 hour 3 min.

How to use it?

  1. Place Tablet(s) in mouth and swallow with water.

Dosage

  1. For the management of pain, the recommended dose is 1 or 2 tablets every 4 to 6 hours as needed for pain relief up to a maximum of 8 tablets per day. The dosing interval should not be less than six hours.

How to store it?

  1. Store in a cool and dry place and keep away from children.
